About Us

BERAL CONSULTING GROUP is a trusted brand with more than 20 years of experience in the local and international markets. Committed to making a difference, our team is predominantly composed of skilled women professionals. We are dedicated to offering enhanced opportunities for professional development and employment, particularly to women. Our mission extends to empowering working mothers, enabling them to pursue their careers while maintaining a strong presence in their families.

Moreover, we are passionate about narrowing the gap faced by immigrants in English-speaking countries, particularly concerning access to healthcare and legal services due to language barriers.
